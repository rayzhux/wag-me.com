FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions

What software companies does WAG operate?

WAG operates three software companies: AutoData (automotive data and analytics APIs serving OEMs, insurers, and government), Vicimus (dealer software solutions for digital marketing and inventory management), and Axxion (insurance claims management platform for the GCC market).

What is AutoData?

AutoData is WAG's automotive data and analytics company providing APIs for vehicle valuations, specifications, market intelligence, and risk scoring. AutoData serves OEMs, dealers, insurers, banks, and government agencies across the GCC region.

What does Vicimus do?

Vicimus is a dealer software solutions company that provides digital marketing, inventory management, and customer retention tools for automotive dealerships and OEMs. Originally founded in Canada, Vicimus now serves dealers across North America and the Middle East.
