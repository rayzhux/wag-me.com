Build the Next Generation of Automotive Businesses

The Opportunity

Why a Venture Studio

Idea — Automotive × Technology
Validate — Data + Customer Signal
Build — MVP on WAG Platform
Standalone WAG Business

The MENA automotive ecosystem is fragmented, largely offline, and underserved by technology. This is not a problem solved by one product — it is solved by building many businesses, fast, on shared infrastructure.

Building a new business and running a scaled business require fundamentally different management systems. The Venture Studio provides the former — the environment, resources, data, and capital to go from idea to product-market fit at speed.

Every venture benefits from WAG's existing data assets, customer relationships, physical network, and institutional capital — advantages that would take years and millions to replicate independently.

How It Works

From idea to independent business

1

You Own It

Founders retain ownership and drive the vision. WAG provides the platform, not the playbook. You build the business your way, with our infrastructure behind you.

2

Stage-Gated, Evidence-Driven

Progress through clear milestones. Each stage is defined by evidence of customer demand, product-market fit, and unit economics — not promises or projections.

3

More Evidence = More Resources

Capital, team, and platform access scale with demonstrated traction. Prove demand and we invest more. Clear gates prevent waste and ensure focus.

4

Graduate to Full WAG Business

Successful ventures graduate into standalone WAG businesses with their own P&L, leadership team, and operational independence within the WAG ecosystem.
