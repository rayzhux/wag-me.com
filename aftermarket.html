THE INFLECTION POINT

Fuel Retail Is at a Crossroads

The global fuel retail industry is being reshaped by electrification, margin compression, and changing customer expectations. Forward-thinking operators are turning stations into mobility hubs.

The Challenge

  • →Structural pressure on fuel margins from regulation and EV transition
  • →Volume growth uncertainty as mobility patterns shift
  • →Forecourt assets generating below potential revenue per sqm

The Opportunity

  • →Quick service is the highest-margin non-fuel category at fuel stations
  • →Built-in customer traffic from fuel operations creates natural demand
  • →EV servicing opens entirely new revenue streams at the same location
Business Opportunity At A Glance
Quick service represents the most strategic non-fuel growth engine for forward-thinking fuel retailers — turning existing station footprints into high-margin mobility service hubs.

Partnership Models

Franchise

Best for DO-Stations

Rapid deployment with revenue share structure. WAG provides the brand, technology, training, and operational playbooks. Ideal for dealer-operated stations seeking a proven format.

  • Revenue share model
  • Minimal capex for partner
  • Fastest time-to-launch

Joint Venture

Best for COCO Stations

Shared investment with aligned incentives. Perfect for company-owned, company-operated flagship locations and strategic market entries where both parties invest.

  • Shared equity & governance
  • Deep strategic alignment
  • Premium brand experience

Build-Operate-Transfer

Flexible Framework

WAG builds and operates, then transfers to the partner after a defined period. Customized risk allocation with long-term operational framework and flexible exit mechanisms.

  • Low partner risk at start
  • Proven before transfer
  • Flexible exit options
