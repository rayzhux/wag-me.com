FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions

What is WAG's automotive fintech venture?

WAG is building a digital-first F&I (Finance and Insurance) brokerage — an API-driven marketplace that connects car buyers, dealers, banks, and insurers on a single platform. The goal is to reduce auto finance approval times from 10+ days to 48 hours in the Middle East market.

Why is there an opportunity in automotive fintech in the Middle East?

The used car market in the Middle East is 7-8x larger than new car sales, yet only 4 players control 85% of auto finance. Most buyers face 10+ day approval processes, 30% F&I penetration rates, and 60% of dealers lack any digital F&I support — creating a massive underserved market.

What stage is WAG's fintech product?

WAG's automotive fintech venture is currently in the Venture Studio development phase (Phase 1), building the core API marketplace platform. The product is being developed as part of WAG's Venture Studio model, which validates, builds, and scales new automotive technology businesses.
