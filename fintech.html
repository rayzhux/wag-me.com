MARKET OPPORTUNITY

A Market Waiting to Be Digitized

The used car market is 7-8x larger than the new car market, with even lower F&I penetration. Only 4 major players control 85% of the market — all running legacy models.

No one has built a digital-first platform for automotive F&I in the region. No focused near-prime/subprime player exists in consumer automotive finance. Banks’ rising origination costs create natural demand for a platform aggregator.
