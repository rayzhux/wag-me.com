## Build With Us

WAG isn't just an automotive company. It's a venture studio that builds new businesses at the intersection of automotive, data, AI, and financial services across the Middle East and Africa.

We combine 40+ years of hard-won automotive expertise with a technology-first platform and the capital to back founders who want to build the next generation of automotive and mobility businesses in the region.

## Why a Venture Studio

The MENA automotive ecosystem is fragmented, largely offline, and underserved by technology. That's not a problem you solve with one product -- it's a problem you solve by building many businesses, fast, on top of shared infrastructure.

WAG's Venture Studio is how we do that. We take the best ideas -- whether they come from inside WAG or from external founders and entrepreneurs -- and give them the resources, data, customers, and operational backing to prove themselves quickly or fail cheaply.

The philosophy: building a new business and running a scaled business require fundamentally different management systems. The Venture Studio provides the former. Discipline without bureaucracy. Speed without recklessness. Conviction backed by evidence.

## What We Provide

Founders who build with WAG don't start from zero. They start with:

**Proprietary data assets.** AutoData is the GCC's leading automotive data provider -- vehicle identification, valuations, analytics, and AI-powered intelligence across UAE, KSA, and Oman. Your venture can plug into this from day one.

**330,000+ existing customer relationships.** Through FastTrack and ExpressFit, WAG serves hundreds of thousands of vehicle owners annually across 32+ locations in the UAE. Real customers, real transactions, real feedback loops.

**A physical service network.** Quick service locations across the UAE, with expansion into Saudi Arabia, Egypt, and Morocco. If your venture needs physical touchpoints, we have them.

**Technology and AI platform.** Shared infrastructure including APIs, data pipelines, AI capabilities, and product standards. You build your product; we provide the platform layer underneath it.

**Institutional backing and capital.** Ring-fenced budget for your venture, a dedicated team, and a leadership group that has built and exited automotive businesses across multiple continents. This isn't advisory support -- it's operational backing from people who have run real P&Ls.

**Cross-vertical distribution.** WAG operates across data & software, insurance, aftermarket services, and financial services. Your venture can sell into and build on top of multiple verticals from the start.

## Who We're Looking For

**Entrepreneurs and founders** with a concept that needs automotive data, distribution, customer relationships, or regional expertise to scale. If you've built something that works but need WAG's assets to take it region-wide, we want to talk.

**Operators with domain expertise** in automotive, insurance, fintech, or mobility who are ready to stop advising and start building. If you know the problem cold and want to own the solution end-to-end, this is for you.

**Small teams or early-stage products** that WAG could absorb and scale. If you have an MVP and need distribution, data, and capital to grow, the Venture Studio is your launchpad.

## What a Venture Looks Like at WAG

You own it. A Venture Lead at WAG is not a project manager or a department head with a side project. You are the CEO of your venture within WAG's ecosystem. You get a dedicated squad, a ring-fenced budget, and direct access to WAG's executive team and shared platform.

The process is stage-gated and evidence-driven. We start small, test fast, and scale what works. More evidence unlocks more resources. If the evidence says stop, we stop -- and that's the system working, not a failure. When a venture proves repeatable economics, it graduates into a full WAG business with its own P&L. The Venture Lead becomes the Managing Director.

## The Culture of Building

WAG runs on a simple principle: anyone can propose a new venture idea. The best ideas don't come from the top of an org chart -- they come from people closest to the customer problem. We evaluate ideas on evidence and market signal, not seniority or politics.

We kill most ideas early and cheaply. That's intentional. A healthy venture pipeline generates many concepts and focuses resources on the few that show real signal. The ideas that don't make it today go into a bank and get revisited as market conditions change.

What we don't do: let promising concepts die slowly as side projects inside existing businesses. Every venture that passes initial screening gets dedicated ownership and resources. That's non-negotiable.

## The Compound Advantage

Every venture built inside WAG makes every other WAG business stronger:

- AutoData's vehicle intelligence improves aftermarket diagnostics and F&I risk scoring
- Axxion's claims data feeds into better insurance products and aftermarket repair demand
- Aftermarket customer relationships create F&I cross-sell opportunities
- New ventures generate new data that flows back into the platform

This is the flywheel. Data leads to smarter decisions, which leads to better outcomes, which leads to more transactions, which leads to more data. Your venture doesn't just benefit from the platform -- it makes the platform better for everyone.
