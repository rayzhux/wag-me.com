## Why Join WAG

### The Opportunity
WAG is at an inflection point: 40+ years of proven automotive expertise being rebuilt as a technology-driven platform company. This is not a mature corporation maintaining the status quo -- it's a team of operators building something new, with the resources and track record to make it real.

### What Makes WAG Different as an Employer

**Startup mindset, institutional backing.** WAG operates with small teams, fast cycles, and real ownership. But unlike a typical startup, there's 40 years of domain expertise, established customer relationships, and real revenue behind the ambition. You get the speed and autonomy of a startup with the resources and stability of a proven business.

**Real P&L ownership.** Leaders at WAG don't manage processes -- they run businesses. Every vertical, every venture has a clear owner accountable for outcomes, not activities.

**Multi-continental, multi-vertical exposure.** WAG operates across UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Canada, and expanding into Egypt and Morocco. Across data/software, fintech, aftermarket, and knowledge management. Working here means exposure to multiple industries, markets, and stages of business maturity.

**Build, don't maintain.** Through the Venture Studio, WAG is actively creating new businesses. For builders, this means the chance to take something from zero to one -- with evidence-based support and real resources.

**Technology at the center.** AI and data are not bolt-on initiatives at WAG -- they're core to every product and operating decision. The shared platform layer means technology builders serve multiple verticals, not one siloed project.

**Dubai HQ, regional impact.** Headquartered in Abu Dhabi (ADGM), operating across the GCC's fastest-growing automotive markets. For people who want to build in the Middle East with global standards, this is the place.

## Culture Principles (derived from leadership orientation and company docs)

1. **Evidence over opinions.** Decisions are anchored in data and measurable proof, not hierarchy or tradition.
2. **Execution, execution, execution.** Strategy without shipping is worthless. WAG values people who build and deliver.
3. **Work smart, not just hard.** Validate, invest, and create returns at every step -- don't wait years to prove something works.
4. **Freedom with accountability.** Autonomy to run your domain, accountability for results.
5. **Learn from everything.** Cross-vertical learning loops, knowledge capture, and decision traceability -- so what you build compounds for the whole organization.

## Who WAG is Looking For

WAG's leadership team comes from:
- Global automotive OEMs (Audi, VW, GM, SAIC, Geely)
- Consumer and energy multinationals (Procter & Gamble, TotalEnergies, ADNOC)
- Insurance and fintech (AXA, Daman, Qatar Insurance Group)
- Technology across China, North America, and the Middle East

The pattern: operators and builders who have shipped real products, run real P&Ls, and been accountable for outcomes in complex, multi-market environments. People who are excited about a blank canvas, not just a defined role.
