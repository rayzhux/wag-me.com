## Vertical Overview

The intelligence layer of WAG. APIs, AI-driven analytics, dealer software, and claims platforms serving OEMs, dealers, insurers, financers, and government.

This vertical houses three operating companies: AutoData, Vicimus, and Axxion. Together they form the data and technology backbone that powers WAG's other verticals and serves external customers across the automotive ecosystem.

## AutoData Middle East

### What It Is
AutoData is WAG's automotive data and analytics platform. It is the GCC's leading automotive data solutions provider, delivering precise vehicle intelligence and scalable B2B and B2C solutions built for accuracy and transparency.

Established in 2010, AutoData pioneered policy issuance, claims management, and vehicle history reporting solutions in the region.

### How It Describes Itself (from website)
"AI-Powered Automotive Analytics, Valuations & Warranty Solutions. Accurate & Trusted Automotive Data. Anytime."

### Products & Services

**AutoData Web Services (ADWS)**
API-first web services providing vehicle identification, valuation, and history data. Automotive data at your fingertips for insurers and automotive businesses.

**AutoData Analytics**
AI-driven analytics including residual values, price forecasting, market supply indicators, and real-time reporting. Data-driven business intelligence for automotive businesses.

**DealRevs**
Dealer inventory, pricing, and turn optimization -- building toward vAuto-grade rigor with embedded performance management. Maximizes profitability in used car sales.

**Carantee**
White-label software for vehicle warranty and certification programs.

**Car Valuation Service (CVS)**
Accurate vehicle appraisals for optimal pricing, primarily serving insurers.

**Vehicle Report**
Detailed vehicle history reports for buyers and sellers.

### Data Sources & Partnerships
- Built on proprietary regional datasets
- Global partnerships with JATO Dynamics (global vehicle specifications data)
- CARFAX integration (vehicle history)
- OtoFacts / EVG (Emirates Vehicle Gate) for local vehicle history
- Data from web retailers, auction houses, and proprietary collection
- Coverage across UAE, KSA, and Oman (expanding to Kuwait, Bahrain, Qatar)

### Awards & Recognition
- 2025: "Best AI-Powered Automotive Valuation Platform - UAE"
- 2025: "Best Data Intelligence Solution for Automotive Financing - UAE"

### Why It Matters (Key Value Proposition)
AutoData turns Quick Service from a labor-driven activity into a data-driven, scalable profit engine. Across the ecosystem:
- Precise vehicle identification enables faster diagnostics and higher technician productivity
- Data-driven service recommendations increase conversion and average ticket
- Objective market data protects customer trust and repeat visits
- Standardized intelligence allows Quick Service to scale consistently across sites

It offers services from digital marketing to custom software development, helping automotive businesses increase sales, service, and parts business through custom-developed digital marketing tools.

### How It Describes Itself (from website)
"Automotive Tech Solutions Driving Success for Dealerships. Customer Lifecycle Management that Converts for Every Department."

## Axxion Insurance Services

### What It Is
Axxion is the UAE's first bumper-to-bumper Third-Party Administrator (TPA) for motor claims. It's a compliance-grade motor claims operating system that reduces claims costs and improves customer satisfaction with full transparency.

Axxion converts insurance claims into a managed, auditable supply channel that lifts revenue, protects margin, and improves cash conversion.

### How It Describes Itself (from website)
"Claims Operating System. Institutional-grade motor claims management. Compliance by design."

### Core Services
- Claims management with end-to-end audit trails
- AI damage estimation and fraud detection
- Vehicle surveying and quality assurance
- Loss adjusting
- Salvage management and spare parts management
- Customer service with AI-powered communications
- "Golden Garage" governed repair network

### Technology: Axxion Claim OS
A 6-layer system with:
- 7 Compliance Gates (Registration, Triage, Allocation, Estimate, Quality, Hand-back, Settlement)
- 176-point FNOL data registration
- 50+ point quality control checklist
- 110+ garage assessment checklist per workshop
- 17 structured data tables per claim
- End-to-end immutable audit trail with full change log
- Real-time dashboards for cycle time, cost, and SLA monitoring

### AI Capabilities (Human-in-the-Loop)
- AI video assessment for remote damage classification and cost estimation
- Document processing (Emirates ID, registration, license, police report extraction)
- Fraud screening across damage photos, claim history, claimant profiles, workshop patterns
- Estimate validation against price book and benchmark data
- Predictive workshop routing with weighted scoring
- Automated customer communications with human override

### Quantified Value
- 50% operational claims handling reductions
- 7-10% guaranteed claims cost reductions
- Cycle time improvement: 3-10 days vs. 5-15 days industry average
- NPS improvement from 55 to 75 (+20 points)

### Security & Compliance
SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, ISO 9001, AES-256 encryption, UAE PDPL compliance, 10-year records retention. Aligned with new CBUAE Law (Circulars 24/2022, 25/2022) effective September 2026.

### Provider Network
- 51 workshops mapped and assessed across 5 UAE Emirates
- 110+ data points per workshop assessment
- 100+ vehicle brands mapped against workshop capabilities
- Six-stage provider lifecycle: Prospect, Vetting, Contracting, Active, Probation, Blacklisting

### Who It Serves
- For Insurers: Lower repair costs, faster cycle times, audit-ready and compliant operations
- For Repairers: Steady work volume, fair pricing, clear standards; governed repair network rewards quality workshops

### Key Differentiator
No financial relationship between workshop and claims handler. Workshop selection based on capability, quality, and cost -- not opaque arrangements. Every cost element verified against independent market benchmarks.
