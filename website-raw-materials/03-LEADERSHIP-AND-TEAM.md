## Team Narrative for Website

The WAG leadership team is not a collection of consultants or career managers. It's a group of operators and builders who have run real businesses at scale -- dealerships, insurance companies, fuel retail networks, technology platforms -- across multiple continents. The common thread: everyone here has built something, shipped it, and been accountable for the P&L.

**Where the team comes from:**
- Audi, VW, GM, SAIC, Geely (automotive OEMs)
- Procter & Gamble, TotalEnergies, ADNOC (global consumer/energy)
- AXA, Daman, Qatar Insurance Group, AI Wathba (insurance/fintech)
- Technology leaders from China, North America, and the Middle East

**What makes the team different:**
- Operator DNA, not advisory DNA
- Cross-continental experience (GCC, Europe, North America, Asia)
- Mix of deep automotive domain expertise and technology-first thinking
- Small team, high ownership -- every leader runs a real P&L
