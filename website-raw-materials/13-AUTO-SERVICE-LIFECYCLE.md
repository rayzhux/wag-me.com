## The Automotive Service Lifecycle

WAG's platform spans the full vehicle ownership journey. This framework shows where WAG's products and services fit:

### 1. Discover & Decide
Compare models, pricing, features. WAG provides: vehicle data, market intelligence, pricing analytics (AutoData).

### 2. Acquire (New / Used / Import)
Test drive, negotiate, purchase. WAG provides: dealer software, inventory management, digital marketing (Vicimus, DealRevs).

### 3. Legalize (Make it Road-Legal)
Registration, inspection, insurance. WAG provides: vehicle identification, valuation certification, inspection services (AutoData, Carantee).

### 4. Pay & Protect
Financing, insurance selection, renewal. WAG provides: F&I marketplace (future), vehicle valuation for lending, claims management (Axxion).

### 5. Operate (Daily Life)
Fuel, charging, tolls, parking, subscriptions. WAG touches: fuel retail partnerships (ExpressFit at fuel stations).

### 6. Maintain & Repair
Scheduled/unscheduled service, parts, warranty. WAG provides: FastTrack QS, ExpressFit, spare parts marketplace, warranty programs (Carantee).

### 7. Incidents & Sell/Trade
Accidents, repairs, resale, trade-in. WAG provides: claims management (Axxion), vehicle history reports, valuations (AutoData), remarketing support.

## Why This Matters

Most automotive companies serve one or two steps. WAG is building integrated solutions across the entire lifecycle -- with data flowing between every step. This is the compound advantage:

- Vehicle data from Step 1 improves insurance pricing in Step 4
- Service history from Step 6 increases resale value in Step 7
- Claims data from Step 7 improves fraud detection in Step 4
- Customer relationships from Step 6 drive F&I cross-sell in Step 4

The flywheel works because every touchpoint generates data that makes every other touchpoint smarter.

## Market Context: Why MENA, Why Now

### The Region's Automotive Ecosystem is Fragmented and Underserved

- Legacy business practices have frustrated customers and created mistrust in the industry
- Processes are largely offline and manual across financing, insurance, service, and remarketing
- Choice, transparency, and trust are the key drivers for behavioral change
- Emerging local innovators validate potential for new technologies and aggregator models

### The Opportunity

- No one has built the comprehensive technology backbone for the automotive economy in MENA
- The market is large enough and fragmented enough for a platform player to create outsized value
- Fuel retail economics under pressure create natural demand for mobility service partnerships
- Customer expectations shifting toward speed, convenience, transparency, and digital journeys
- Platform-led disruption opportunity through brand-agnostic, digitally enabled operations

### WAG's Right to Win

- 40+ years of operational experience in the region
- Proprietary data assets (AutoData) that no competitor can easily replicate
- Existing physical footprint (32+ locations, 330,000+ customers)
- Professional leadership team with global automotive and technology credentials
- Venture studio model for disciplined new business creation
- Capital from decades of successful exits (self-funded conviction)
