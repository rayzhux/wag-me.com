## What is World Automotive Group?

World Automotive Group (WAG) is a technology-driven automotive platform building across MENA and North America. We combine proprietary data, AI, and integrated software with a venture-studio model to launch, scale, and compound new businesses at speed. Our flywheel -- data into smarter decisions into better outcomes into more transactions into more data -- is designed to evolve WAG into a finance-centric ecosystem over time.

## Portfolio Structure: Four Verticals

WAG operates across four verticals -- each technology-enabled, each designed to compound data and network effects across the group:

### 1. Data & Software Solutions
The intelligence layer -- APIs, AI-driven analytics, dealer software, and claims platforms serving OEMs, dealers, insurers, financers, and government.

### 2. Fintech Automotive Financial Services
Digital-first brokerage for financing and insurance -- B2B and B2C. Building what doesn't yet exist in the region.

### 3. Aftermarket Solutions
Scaled vehicle ownership services (quick service, parts, inspections) with online + offline reach and tight supply chain integration.

### 4. Knowledge Management & Learning
The system that captures what WAG learns and turns it into repeatable playbooks, training, and cross-vertical improvement loops.
