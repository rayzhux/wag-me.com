# WAG Corporate Website - Vertical: Knowledge Management & Learning

> Compiled from WAG Company.md and board meeting materials.

---

## Vertical Overview

Turn everything WAG builds, learns, and operates into a scalable knowledge system -- so expertise compounds across the group instead of staying locked in individual teams.

## What We're Building

**Operating playbooks and execution standards:** Codified processes for how WAG launches, scales, and governs ventures and operations -- from Venture Studio stage gates to site-level P&L management.

**Training and onboarding platform:** Structured programs that get new team members productive fast, whether they're joining a scaled business or an early-stage venture.

**Knowledge base and decision traceability:** A living system that captures key decisions, the evidence behind them, and the outcomes -- so the organization learns from itself.

**Cross-vertical learning loops:** Mechanisms that route insights from one vertical (e.g., aftermarket site performance) into others (e.g., fintech risk models), accelerating the entire group.

## Why This is a Vertical, Not Just an Internal Function

WAG operates across multiple countries, verticals, and stages of maturity. The ability to systematically capture and redeploy knowledge is a competitive advantage -- and over time, it can become a product (advisory, licensing, training) for the broader automotive ecosystem.

## Relevance for Website

This vertical signals institutional maturity to partners and potential employees. It tells government entities and large corporates: "We don't just execute -- we codify, measure, and improve systematically." For talent, it says: "Join a company where what you build and learn gets captured and amplified, not lost."
