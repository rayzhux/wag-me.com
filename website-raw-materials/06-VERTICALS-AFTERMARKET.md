## ExpressFit

### What It Is
ExpressFit is WAG's next-generation quick-service automotive concept, purpose-built for fuel retail partnerships across GCC, Middle East, and Northern Africa. It is the premium brand dedicated to scaling WAG's proven aftermarket operations into new markets and new partnerships.

Building on 40+ years of success operating vehicle service workshops in the GCC, ExpressFit represents the evolution from FastTrack's proven model into a standardized, high-throughput format with validated unit economics.

### Vision
For ExpressFit to be the brand providing a platform to fuel retailers for all aftermarket automotive services, recognized by customer experience and preference as the most professional, clean, reliable, and reputable.

### Services
- Quick Maintenance (oil changes, routine services)
- Diagnostics (complete technical inspections)
- Light Repairs (mechanical services and part replacement)
- Tires & Brakes (installation, alignment, repair)
- Service HVAC (heating, ventilation, air conditioning)
- ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems)
- Car Wash (automated and manual)
- Electric Vehicle Maintenance (batteries and fluids)

### Three Scalable Formats

**1. XS - Express Format (2 bays, <150 sqm)**
- Oil, filters, batteries
- High-throughput urban and commuter sites
- Minimal capex, fast payback

**2. Core Quick Service Format (4-6 bays, 200-300 sqm)**
- QS + car care
- Balanced economics
- Optimal replication format across network

**3. Lighthouse Format (6-8+ bays, 400+ sqm)**
- Premium CX, training, and brand showcase
- Strategic high-visibility locations
- Top tier EBITDA and cash flow contribution

Each format optimized for limited real estate and variable traffic flows, delivering superior incremental EBITDA versus passive rental approaches.

### Digital Solutions Platform
ExpressFit comes with a comprehensive technology platform:
- Workshop Management System (real-time job tracking, scheduling, technician productivity, inventory management, digital service history)
- Customer Relationship Management (customer database, automated appointment reminders, loyalty programs, targeted marketing campaigns)
- Flexible Payment Solutions ("Service Now, Pay Later," multiple payment methods, installment plans)
- Smart Procurement Platform (automated parts ordering, supplier network integration, real-time pricing and availability, inventory optimization)

### Target Audiences
- Busy vehicle owners and families seeking fast, reliable service
- Businesses with vehicle fleets requiring regular, affordable maintenance

### Scale Targets
- 100+ locations across 10 MEA countries by 2028
- 360,000+ vehicles when fully launched

### Led By
Thomas Rebeyrol, CEO of WAG Aftermarket Solutions (background: Procter & Gamble, TotalEnergies, ADNOC)

## The Fuel Retail Partnership Model

### Why Fuel Retailers Need WAG

The fuel retail industry faces a structural inflection point:
- Structural pressure on fuel margins
- Long-term volume growth uncertainty
- C-store differentiation increasingly difficult
- Forecourt assets require higher value per square meter

Quick service represents the most strategic non-fuel growth value-pool engine for forward-thinking fuel retailers. Mobility services are emerging as the most resilient non-fuel revenue stream.

### What WAG Offers Fuel Retail Partners

**Partnership-led, capital-efficient operating model designed specifically for fuel retail networks.**

Fuel Retail Partner Provides:
- Sites, traffic, utilities, and brand environment
- Integration into forecourt and loyalty ecosystem
- Governance and strategic oversight
- Network access and customer base

WAG Provides:
- Format design, layouts, and customer journeys
- Day-to-day operations: staffing, training, procurement
- Digital platform and performance management
- Operational excellence and continuous improvement
- Digital tailor-made innovations

### Why Choose WAG
- 20+ years of Quick Service execution
- Platform designed to integrate seamlessly with fuel retail operations
- Long-term alignment with capital efficiency focus
- Risk and execution allocated to the best-equipped party
- Proven model that transforms forecourt assets into high-value mobility service hubs

### Flexible Partnership Structures

**1. Franchise Model**
- Rapid deployment capability
- Revenue share structure
- Ideal for network-wide rollout beyond COCO (perfect fit for DO-stations)
- Ideal for franchising stand-alone sites

**2. Joint Venture**
- Enhanced alignment on strategic and financial objectives
- Perfect fit for operated CO-stations and flagship locations
- Shared investment and returns
- Fuel retailer perceives land lease from the JV
- Premium site sustainable NFR development

**3. (Co-)Managed / BOT Structures**
- Build-Operate-Transfer options
- Customized risk allocation
- Long-term partnership framework
- Flexible exit strategies

### Current & Target Partners
- UAE: ADNOC, Emarat (existing)
- KSA: Expansion targets (Aramco ecosystem, Total)
- MEA: Shell, Petrom, and others in pipeline
- Egypt: Great Automotive Group acquisition (400 stations across Chill Out and Watania networks)
- Morocco: Agreement in principle with Petron and Petramine
