## Vertical Overview

Digital-first brokerage for financing and insurance -- B2B and B2C. WAG is building what doesn't yet exist in the region: a comprehensive, API-driven F&I engine with deep bank and insurer integrations.

This vertical is currently progressing through WAG's Venture Studio pipeline -- validating demand, building early proof points, and designing the product architecture.

## Why This Vertical Exists

The regional F&I market is ripe for disruption. The current state:
- Fragmented processes with manual workflows
- Slow cycle times (10+ days from application to approval in many cases)
- Low dealer program penetration (only 30% of dealers have sophisticated F&I programs)
- 60% of the market receives no F&I support at all
- Customers experience friction, opacity, and frustration throughout the financing journey
- No one has built a digital-first platform for automotive F&I in the region

## The Problem WAG is Solving

The current F&I experience in the region breaks at every step. A customer buying a car:
- Spends hours on paperwork and repetitive data entry
- Gets bounced between salesperson and "numbers guy," losing all purchase excitement
- Faces 3-10 day approval cycles with no visibility
- Encounters multiple wet signatures (historically 17+ per transaction)
- Has no data retained between transactions -- buying a second car means starting from scratch
- Faces limited product choice and no competitive comparison

The result: 12-15% cancellation rates from process friction alone, 35% rejection rates, and a customer experience so painful it actively destroys value.

## What WAG is Building

A scalable, API-driven F&I marketplace that:
- Connects customers, dealers, banks, and insurers on a single digital platform
- Delivers instant pre-approval before customers even select a specific vehicle
- Reduces approval cycles from 10+ days to target of 48 hours
- Integrates with Credit Bureau, Ministry of Interior, RTA, and 20+ financial institutions via APIs
- Provides menu-driven financing with real-time monthly payment calculations
- Captures and retains customer data across the full lifecycle

## Long-Term Vision

This becomes the embedded-finance layer across the entire WAG platform. The evolution:
- Start as a broker making commissions on transactions
- Become a platform making transaction fees
- Evolve to a principal making spread (holding paper, issuing insurance directly)

WAG's proprietary data and operating footprint enable better underwriting, faster cycle times, and higher product penetration than any standalone fintech.

## Market Opportunity (Public Context)

- Used car market is 7-8x larger than new car market with even lower F&I penetration
- No focused near-prime/subprime player in consumer automotive finance in the region
- No digital platform integrating the full F&I journey end-to-end
- Only 4 major players doing 85% of the market -- all with legacy models
- Banks' origination cost problem creates natural demand for a platform aggregator

## Unique Competitive Advantages

1. **Automotive dealership DNA:** WAG was born from dealership experience and understands F&I deeply -- not just implementing banking APIs
2. **Proprietary data:** AutoData's vehicle intelligence enables better pricing, valuation, and risk assessment
3. **Operating footprint:** FastTrack/ExpressFit network provides physical touchpoints and customer relationships
4. **Customer-centric model:** Makes money when customers get good outcomes, not from advertising manipulation
5. **Cross-vertical synergies:** Aftermarket data feeds into better F&I risk models; F&I products sold inside the service journey
