## Partnership Philosophy

WAG is open to strategic partnerships, joint ventures, and acquisitions. The company operates with an "operator-first mindset" -- WAG builds and runs the business, while partners contribute their assets, networks, and strategic positioning.

All partnership structures are designed to reinforce trust, data ownership, and network relevance while delivering superior incremental EBITDA.
