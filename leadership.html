LEADERSHIP

Operators Who Build. Executives Who Ship.

Automotive executives who have started companies, run P&Ls, and shipped products across North America, Europe, China, and the Middle East.

World Automotive Group's leadership team has been assembled from the world's leading automotive and industrial companies — Audi, Volkswagen, General Motors, SAIC, Wuling, Mercedes-Benz, Al Futtaim, Toyota, Hyundai, Lexus, Procter & Gamble, TotalEnergies, and ADNOC.

What unites us is operator DNA. Every member of the leadership team has built, scaled, or turned around real businesses. We bring cross-continental experience spanning China, North America, Europe, and the Middle East — and a shared conviction that the automotive economy is ready for a technology-driven transformation.
